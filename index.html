Mystic Hazel's Memory Magic

In a whimsical forest nestled deep within an enchanted realm, there lived a charming and thoughtful witch named Hazel. She was known far and wide for her heartwarming spells and ever-present smile, but there was one little problem. Hazel had a memory like a leaky bucket.

As a talented potion maker, she decided to fix her poor memory once and for all by creating a memory-boosting elixir! But Hazel can't remember the ingredients to make it. Can you help her find the matching ingredients scattered around her workshop?

How to Play

  • The game board consists of a grid of facedown cards, each hiding an ingredient.
  • To play, click on any two cards to reveal what's hidden beneath.
  • If the two cards you've chosen reveal matching ingredients, congratulations! You've found a pair.
  • If the cards don't match, don't worry. The cards will automatically flip back face-down, and you can continue your search for pairs.
  • Continue revealing cards and trying to match pairs. The more pairs you find, the closer Hazel becomes to improving her memory.

Winning the Game

  • Once you've successfully matched all the pairs of ingredients, Hazel will be able to create her memory-enhancing potion, and you win the game!

Tips

  • Concentration is key. Pay attention to the cards you've already revealed and where you saw matching ingredients.
  • Keep an eye on the number of moves you make. Try to find all the pairs in as few moves as possible.
  • Challenge yourself to improve your time and earn yourself three stars.
